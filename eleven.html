ELEVEN

Eleven is a powerful girl who escaped from Hawkins Lab. She has special abilities that allow her to move objects with her mind and enter the Upside Down.

Powers

  • Telekinesis
  • Remote viewing
  • Opening portals
  • Mind strength

Personality

  • Brave
  • Loyal to friends
  • Protective
  • Emotionally strong

Role in the Series

Eleven saves Hawkins many times by using her powers. She is the key to stopping monsters from the Upside Down.
