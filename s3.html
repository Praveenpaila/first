Overview

Season 3 is set in 1985 when Starcourt Mall opens in Hawkins. While the town looks normal, a dangerous secret is hidden underground. The Upside Down begins to affect people in the real world.

Main Events

  • Strange behavior in Hawkins people
  • Hidden Russian laboratory discovered
  • Mind Flayer returns in physical form
  • Billy becomes possessed
  • The group investigates Starcourt

Main Characters

  • Eleven
  • Mike Wheeler
  • Dustin Henderson
  • Lucas Sinclair
  • Max Mayfield
  • Steve Harrington
  • Robin Buckley
  • Jim Hopper

Main Villain

The Mind Flayer uses infected humans to build a powerful monster. It grows stronger by controlling the minds and bodies of people.

Climax

In the final battle at Starcourt Mall, Eleven uses all of her strength to fight the Mind Flayer. The Russian machine is destroyed, which closes the gate. Billy sacrifices his life to save Eleven. After the explosion, Hopper is believed to be dead. The group breaks apart as Eleven leaves Hawkins.

Ending Impact

The town is saved, but the group loses Hopper and is emotionally separated. A new danger is teased when a prisoner is shown in Russia at the end.
