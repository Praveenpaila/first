Overview

Season 4 takes place after the group is separated. A new dark force appears in Hawkins causing mysterious murders. A powerful villain called Vecna rises from the Upside Down.

Main Events

  • Strange murders begin in Hawkins
  • Vecna attacks people through their minds
  • Max becomes a target
  • Eleven loses her powers
  • Hopper is revealed alive in Russia
  • Group travels across states to discover the truth

Main Characters

  • Eleven
  • Mike Wheeler
  • Dustin Henderson
  • Lucas Sinclair
  • Max Mayfield
  • Steve Harrington
  • Nancy Wheeler
  • Jim Hopper

Main Villain

Vecna is a former human turned monster. He kills using fear and past memories. He is revealed to be created by Hawkins Lab.

Climax

In the final battle, the group attacks Vecna in the Upside Down. Max is severely injured while being targeted. Although Eleven defeats Vecna, he escapes into the Upside Down. The gate is not fully closed and Hawkins begins to collapse.

Ending Impact

Hawkins is left cracked open. Dark clouds from the Upside Down enter the town. The world is now connected to the other dimension.
